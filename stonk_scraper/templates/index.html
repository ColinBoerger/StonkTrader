Purpose

This website is designed to let people know the top stocks that are being mentioned on reddit. It is a tool designed to make this data easily accessible to everyone who is interested and not just people with the resources to skim reddit everyday. This code is open source

Methodology

I look at the hot posts on reddit and look through the post and comments for stock tickers. Certain tickers have been removed because they are too common in everyday language. See the github for the list of excluded tickers
